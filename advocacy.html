Advocacy

The PSP Association is committed to advocating for policies and regulations that foster innovation, inclusion, and security in Nepal’s digital payment ecosystem. Through policy briefs and regulatory updates, we engage with stakeholders to shape a supportive environment for Payment Service Providers.
